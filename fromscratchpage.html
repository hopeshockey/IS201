Lunch

Dinner

    1. Pan cook chicken with taco seasoning
    2. Pan cook onions and bell peppers
    3. Top with avocado with cumin, salt, and pepper. Add salsa and lettuce

Snacks

  • Fruits like apples, bananas, oranges, and grapes

  • Nuts like peanuts, walnuts, cashews, and almonds

Tips

  • Season, Season, Season!!!!
  • Buy ingredients that can be used in multiple recipes
  • Avoid eating out, this saves money and keeps your body healthy
